About Kutumb

Kutumb (कुटुंब) means “family”. We are a UK‑based charitable organisation bringing the diverse Indian community together through culture, education and service.

Our Mission

To preserve and share Indian heritage while building bridges across generations and communities.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity — everyone is welcome
  • Education — learning for life
  • Seva — service to the community
  • Joy — celebrating together
